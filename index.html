Questions

Everything clinics ask before booking a call.

Is there a contract?+

No long-term contract to start. You can walk away after the setup is delivered if it is not working for you. We would rather earn a renewal than lock you into one.

Who controls the ad budget?+

You do. Ad spend is paid directly to Meta from your own card, not to us. We manage the campaign, but the account and the budget are yours.

What if a lead is not actually qualified?+

That is the point of pay-per-lead pricing. You are paying for people who fit your criteria and show up, not for clicks or form fills. If a lead does not meet what we agreed on, it does not count.

How long until we see results?+

Most campaigns start producing qualified leads within the first two to three weeks of launch. On your strategy call we will give you a realistic 90-day projection based on your clinic type and market, not a guess.

Is this compliant for healthcare advertising?+

We build every funnel to follow Meta's healthcare advertising policies and keep sensitive health information out of ad forms and tracking. We will walk through exactly what data is and is not collected on your strategy call.

What clinic types do you work with?+

Mental health clinics (psychiatry, TMS, ketamine), ABA therapy providers, and physical therapy clinics. We do not run campaigns outside these three so we can actually stay sharp at each one.
